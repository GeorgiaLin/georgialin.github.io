Introduction

What is the purpose of Xiao Xue Tang User Study?

Xiao Xue Tang is a cultural and creative bookstore located on Gulang Island, Xiamen. From the prospective of Design Psychology and UX, My team and I conducted a one-year in-depth follow-up study to further explore its commercial potential.

What have we achieved?
