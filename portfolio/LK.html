Introduction

What is Lucky Kitty?

Lucky Kitty is an advertising agency that promotes online commercial publication to college students for shops and restaurant near Xiamen University. Wechat Official Account is the major platform that used for its advertisement display. It was founded by me and my partners in 2019 and has received popularity among thousands of students.

Ideation

There is an attempt that I always want to try: connect students in campus with nearby shops. I have noticed that, students, especially freshmen, are always looking for distinctive restaurants and featured stores. At the same time many nearby restaurant owners and shop keepers value college students as their important customers. So I came up with the idea to connect students in campus with nearby shops.
